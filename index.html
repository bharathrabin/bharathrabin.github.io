Hello

I am Bharath, an M.Sc. student in Computer Science at TU Berlin. I completed my undergraduate degree from Jacobs University in January 2017. From February 2017 till December 2019 I worked at Teraki. From January 2017 till April 2018 I worked full time. Thereafter, due to my enrollment at university, I worked 20 hours a week. My interests lie at the intersection of Theoretical Computer Science and Machine Learning. Most of my courses that I have completed in my degree relate to one of these two fields of computing. I recently started blogging about topics that I often think about. Head over and check out my blog. Do contact me if you'd like to chat about these topics.

I am a self-taught DJ and I enjoy mixing bass music, broken beats, house and techno. I am crazy about tennis and football. I am a diehard Liverpool F.C. fan, but I am always down to watch a game of football regardless of which teams are playing.
